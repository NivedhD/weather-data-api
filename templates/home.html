📡API Endpoints

GET /api/v1/<station>/<date>

Temperature for a specific station and date. Add ?unit=fahrenheit for Fahrenheit.

Response 
{
  "station_id": "10",
  "station_name": "VAEXJOE",
  "date": "1988-10-25",
  "temperature": -0.9,
  "unit": "celsius",
  "status": "success"
}
